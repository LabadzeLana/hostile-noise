// Biography

Angus Donald Campbell is a designer, educator, and researcher exploring the role of design in sustainable innovation at the intersection of social, technological, and ecological systems.

With a Doctorate in Development Studies and a Master's in Industrial Design, he spent 18 years at the University of Johannesburg, leading its Industrial Design Department before moving to New Zealand in 2021 as Head of Design at the University of Auckland (UoA). Now an Associate Professor in the School of Design at Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), he teaches Service Design and co-leads the Asian Lifestyle Design Lab (LeNSLab), while retaining an honorary role at UoA. His research focuses on co-designing sustainable products, services, and systems, emphasizing social innovation, lay design, and technology to enhanced human capabilities.
