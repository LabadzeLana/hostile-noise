// Biography

Bostjan Botas Kenda is a graphic designer of books and wayfinding systems. He is a full professor and scientific advisor for visual communication at the University of Ljubljana. He graduated from IED in Milan, Italy. He lectures at the Academy of Fine Arts and Design, the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, and the Faculty of Architecture. He is the founder and partner of studiobotas, a graphic design studio specializing in book design and signage in public spaces. The studio represented Slovenia at the 12th Venice Architecture Biennale. He has received nominations and awards at both the national and international stage.

He headed the commission for the preparatory National Document on Design Policy. He was Vice-Rector for Art and Creativity at the University of Ljubljana and Dean of the Academy of Fine Arts and Design. Botas is co-founder of the multidisciplinary summer school Design+Science. For more than a decade and a half, he has been involved in the implementation and development of an artistic doctorate based on practical research through the CA²RE, ADAPT-r (PRS) programs, and the doctoral program in art at the University of Ljubljana.
